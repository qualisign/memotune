concept

Memotune is a cognitive training tool that targets the user's short-term memory, concentration, and mental speed.

It is inspired by the n-back task, which has been used by neuroscientists to measure aspects of working memory.
Studies have indicated that performing the n-back task may improve fluid intelligence.

Memotune is unique among n-back related tasks in being musically-based. Users must differentiate between musical notes instead of visual cues.
Thus, it may have the potential to serve as an ear-training method for musicians.

instuctions

1. Go to the start section.

2. Make sure your speakers are turned on and click on the grey circle. Be prepared to listen.

3. A sequence of notes will begin playing. Each time you hear a note that is the same as the one played two notes before it, click on the circle.

4. If you are correct, you will receive points. If you are incorrect, or you fail to click when the notes are the same, you will lose a try.
When you are out of tries, the task is over.
